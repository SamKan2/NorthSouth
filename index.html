Meet The Family

We are a new academy in Christchurch specialising in No Gi Jiu Jitsu. We are currently operating 5 days per week in Ferrymead. Jiu Jitsu as a sport is progressing rapidly thanks to the likes of ADCC and the UFC gaining popularity, if you’ve ever thought about giving martial arts a try this a great place to start, no gi Jiu Jitsu has minimal start up costs and does not require a specific uniform, shorts and a shirt are absolutely fine for beginners. We have a range of members from active professional competitors, to day one beginners. We offer a safe and fun environment for all to learn in ! If you’re interested you’ll find us at 25 gatherer street, Phillipstown, Christchurch.
