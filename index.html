What is Jiu Jitsu

Commonly referred to as Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu or just BJJ, Jiu-Jitsu is primarily a ground-based martial art. It focuses on grappling with an opponent to maintain control of them. Where it differentiates from many other martial arts, like karate or boxing, is that it doesn’t involve any striking. The goal of Jiu-Jitsu is to exert control over your opponent, eventually leading them to submit, or tap out.
